## Stroke Data

Habagou imports stroke data from
[hanzi-writer-data](https://github.com/chanind/hanzi-writer-data), pinned in
`scripts/import_stroke_data.py`.

`hanzi-writer-data` is derived from
[Make Me a Hanzi](https://github.com/skishore/makemeahanzi). Make Me a Hanzi's
glyph outlines are distributed under the Arphic Public License. Habagou
propagates that license in [LICENSE-ARPHIC](LICENSE-ARPHIC).

## Runtime Libraries

The browser tracing experience uses [Hanzi Writer](https://hanziwriter.org/),
which is MIT licensed.
